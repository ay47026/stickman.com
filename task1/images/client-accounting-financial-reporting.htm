Client accounting & financial reporting

BDO's client accounting services can put together your year-end financial statements in a timely manner so that you can focus on your business priorities. When you need to report to shareholders and stakeholders we can ensure that your year-end financial statements are compliant with the legislative and accounting requirements. Providing complete and accurate picture of your business's financial situation is an important part of financial reporting and use the latest technology to provide a seamless and integrated service.

Our BDO team sits down with you to listen to you to determine your specific reporting needs and we help you:

·         Process and reconcile your accounting transactions and records

·         Prepare your financial reports, activity statements and income tax supporting schedules for income tax reporting

·         Provide compliant and professional financial statements for shareholder and external stakeholder reporting

·         Cash flow forecasts and budgeting services

·         Present and discuss your financial reports to the company board and shareholders
